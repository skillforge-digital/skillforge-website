The Axioms of Probability

In Forex, **Confluence** is the alignment of multiple technical factors at a single price point. It transforms "Guesses" into "High-Probability Hypotheses." The core axiom is the **Cluster Effect**â€”where independent variables converge to form an institutional Point of Interest (POI).

  • Independent Variables: Why signals must come from different sources (Price Structure, Momentum Indicators, and Fibonacci levels) to be statistically valid.
  • Structural Dominance: Price action structure (HH/HL) always supersedes indicator data. Indicators are secondary confirmation, not primary drivers.

Confluence Stratification

We categorize confluence factors into a hierarchy of importance. A professional setup requires a minimum of 3 factors from different tiers.

Tier 1: Primary

Higher Timeframe Trend Alignment & Key S/R Zones. The "Foundation" of the trade thesis.

Tier 2: Secondary

Price Action Rejection (Pin Bars/Engulfing) & EMA Dynamic Support. The "Trigger" of the entry.

Tier 3: Tertiary

RSI Divergence, Fibonacci 61.8% Retracement, and Session Overlap. The "Precision" filter.

The Orchestration Checklist

Before any capital is deployed, the setup must pass the **Confluence Protocol**. If any mandatory factor is missing, the setup is classified as "Noise" and ignored.

  • HTF Alignment: Is the Daily/4H trend in sync with my 15M entry?
  • Zone Validation: Is price reacting to a historically significant liquidity zone?
  • Signal Integrity: Is there a clear momentum shift (Engulfing) or rejection (Pin Bar)?
  • Risk/Reward Ratio: Is the potential gain at least 3x the risk?
