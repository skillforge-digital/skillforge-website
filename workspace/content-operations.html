Content Operations

Daily Publishing System Through Year-End

This operating model is designed for daily publishing from now until the end of the year across WhatsApp, Discord, Telegram, X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn.

Publishing Rule

Non-negotiable rule: every day must contain one authority signal, one community touchpoint, and one conversion or activation touchpoint. Do not allow any platform to become random or inactive.

Daily Workflow

  1. 08:00 strategy lead confirms the day theme and target audience code.
  2. 09:00 copy and design assets are finalized.
  3. 10:00 WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord community content goes live.
  4. 12:00 Instagram, Facebook, X, and LinkedIn first wave posts go live.
  5. 15:00 TikTok or short-form video is published.
  6. 18:00 engagement pass: replies, comments, prompts, call-to-action reinforcement.
  7. 21:00 performance snapshot and next-day notes are logged.

Content Mix Targets

30% Education and skill value
20% Proof and outcomes

15% Community and culture
