Capability Growth, Hiring, And Internal Evolution

This document turns the current structure visible in the repositories into a stronger leadership, staffing, and capability-development roadmap.

Current Signals From The Repositories

  • Role codes and staff seed data already exist.
  • Multi-role identities are supported, including Director, HOD, Specialist, Digital Marketing, Community Manager, and Support Team.
  • Portal logic already separates command environments by role, which means the culture supports hierarchy and responsibility.
  • There is enough internal structure to move from “brand with tools” to “institution with governance.”

Staff Development Priorities

Operational Development

  • Formalize SOPs for content, support, onboarding, and approvals.
  • Standardize weekly reporting across Academy, Agency, Community, and Marketing.
  • Create a leadership rhythm: daily standup, weekly ops review, monthly strategy review.

Capability Development

  • Train specialists to become faculty and public-facing experts.
  • Train digital marketing staff in campaign operations, copy systems, and analytics.
  • Train support and community staff in retention, moderation, escalation, and customer success.

90-Day Organizational Development Plan

Days 1–30

  • Approve formal org structure.
  • Assign channel owners and content workflow.
  • Standardize role names and reporting lines.
  • Document current team members into one staff register.

Days 31–60

  • Launch weekly executive ops review.
  • Create academy, agency, and content KPI dashboards.
  • Formalize consulting offers and institute concept note.
  • Begin faculty and specialist development paths.

Days 61–90

  • Publish first authority asset or whitepaper.
  • Run first enterprise-style consulting or training offer.
  • Implement progression/nobility framework for recognition.
  • Review staffing gaps and hire for weakest links.
