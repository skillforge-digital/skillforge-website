Recommended Enterprise Structure

Corporate Leadership

  • Founder & CEO: vision, capital relationships, external authority, final strategic decisions.
  • Chief Strategy Officer: brand architecture, growth logic, consulting expansion, board papers, long-range planning.
  • Chief Operating Officer: systems, process discipline, quality assurance, execution management.
  • Chief Finance & Administration Officer: budgeting, controls, contracts, payroll, legal administration.

Operating Divisions

  • SkillForge Academy: training, curriculum, trainees, certification, community learning.
  • SkillForge Agency: delivery, client projects, service contracts, account quality.
  • SkillForge Consulting: enterprise advisory, workshops, transformation programs, B2B training.
  • SkillForge Institute: frameworks, research, whitepapers, standards, academic authority.

Existing Role Logic Found In The Portal

Director

Highest access role in the RBAC model. Controls staff, trainees, audit, announcements, registration dates, and system-level actions.

HOD

Unit leader role. Suited to department operations, trainee management, course coordination, and analytics visibility.

Specialist

Execution and subject-matter role. Best used for track delivery, functional depth, and implementation ownership.

Digital Marketing

Already present in code and seed data. Should evolve into a formal Brand Growth or Content Operations function.

Community Manager

Natural owner for Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp groups, engagement loops, and student/community experience.

Support Team

Currently suited for response, onboarding, password/reset support, and community assistance.
